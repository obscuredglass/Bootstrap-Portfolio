My name is Aaron Hochman. I have Bachelor of Science in Mass Communication from SUNY Oneonta. I'm a musician and documentarian. I've been playing guitar 17 years.

I make electronic and hip-hop of music on Ableton Live & Suite for 8 years while using an APC40 controller makde by Akia. My composition skills got honed using a midi program called Tab-It, where I experimented with layering melodies and figuring out how to make interesting rhythms.

I'm also a painter and digital artist as well. I use oil paint, canvas, and Procreate for iPad. I've been learning how to use Photoshop and Illustrator. I'm currnetly working on pixel art of mostly various pop/weird culture icons.
